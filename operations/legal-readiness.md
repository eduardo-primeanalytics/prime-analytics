# Legal and Commercial Readiness

This is an internal readiness checklist, not legal advice. Agreements should be prepared or reviewed by counsel familiar with US clients and cross-border delivery from Honduras.

- [ ] Confirm the contracting legal entity and public business name.
- [ ] Confirm USD invoicing, payment processor/bank route, tax handling, and payment terms.
- [ ] Prepare a mutual NDA template.
- [ ] Prepare a data processing agreement template.
- [ ] Prepare a master services agreement.
- [ ] Prepare a Revenue Data Blueprint statement of work using the delivery checklist.
- [ ] Prepare an implementation statement of work.
- [ ] Define intellectual-property ownership and pre-existing-material treatment.
- [ ] Define confidentiality, data-security, breach-notification, and deletion obligations.
- [ ] Define subcontractor approval requirements.
- [ ] Define limitation of liability, warranty, termination, and dispute terms with counsel.
- [ ] Make website promises match the executed templates.
- [ ] Re-review documents when entering a regulated industry or handling sensitive personal data.
