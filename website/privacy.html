Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This page explains what data primeanalytics.ai collects, why, and who else touches it. This is a plain-language summary, not a substitute for legal advice — if you need a formal data processing agreement for your own compliance purposes, contact us and we'll put one together.

What we collect directly

If you email us or book a call, we receive whatever you send us: your name, email address, and the content of your message. We use it only to respond to you and, if we start working together, to deliver the engagement. We don't sell or rent this information to anyone.

Analytics

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand traffic to this site. It's cookieless and doesn't track you individually across sites — it reports aggregated metrics like page views and referrers. See Cloudflare's Web Analytics page for details.

Third-party services

  • Calendly — if you book a call, Calendly processes your name, email, and scheduling data under its own privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts — this site loads fonts from Google's servers, which may log your IP address as part of normal web font delivery. See Google's privacy policy.
  • Cloudflare — our hosting provider, which processes standard web request metadata (IP address, request headers) to serve the site and provide security/performance features.

Client data (engagements)

If you become a client, any data pipelines, warehouses, or models we build run on infrastructure you own and control (your Snowflake/BigQuery account, your cloud environment). We don't retain copies of your production data after an engagement ends unless you ask us to. NDAs and data processing agreements are available on request before any engagement begins.

Your rights

You can ask us what data we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it, by emailing hello@primeanalytics.ai.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we'll update the date at the top of this page.
