01 · Executive summary

The decision Harbor & Field cannot make

“Which referral partners and marketing sources produce the most valuable signed engagements?”

The partners remember where some clients came from, HubSpot records a source for others, and finance keeps signed contract values in a monthly spreadsheet. No report connects all three consistently, so the firm cannot decide where to invest its relationship-building time.

Primary gap

Source lives in memory

Referral source is missing or entered as free text for 42% of signed engagements.

Manual burden

Two days every month

An operations manager combines CRM exports, invoices, and a partner-maintained contract sheet by hand.

Recommendation

Keep the current tools

The firm does not need a warehouse. A governed Sheet and cleaner HubSpot process can answer the question.

04 · 30-day roadmap

Fix collection first, then automate the report

WEEK 01 · DEFINE

Agree on the language

  • Approve six source categories
  • Define a signed engagement
  • Assign one reporting owner
WEEK 02 · CAPTURE

Repair the workflow

  • Add required HubSpot fields
  • Standardize deal stages
  • Backfill active opportunities
WEEKS 03–04 · REPORT

Build and hand off

  • Create the governed Sheet
  • Validate against signed contracts
  • Record the refresh walkthrough
05 · Implementation boundary

What the proposed lightweight build includes

Included

  • HubSpot field and deal-stage design
  • Governed Google Sheets reporting model
  • Source, partner, contract value, and conversion reporting
  • Missing-data and reconciliation checks
  • Documentation and recorded handoff

Not included

  • A warehouse or new BI platform
  • Full CRM migration or cleanup
  • Bookkeeping or QuickBooks administration
  • Marketing or referral-program management
  • Ongoing monthly report operation

The right answer does not have to be the biggest system

This fictional example shows how a Blueprint can lead to a simple, dependable solution. The recommendation follows the business question—not a predetermined technology stack.
