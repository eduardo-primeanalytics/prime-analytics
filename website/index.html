Questions we get asked

If yours isn't here, that's what the call is for.

What does a project actually cost?

It depends on scope — a full warehouse-and-dashboard build is a different investment than a lightweight attribution model on data you already have clean. We quote a fixed price after the audit, before you commit to anything, so there's no open-ended bill.

How long does an engagement take?

The audit is one week. The build after that ships in weekly increments you review as it goes — total timeline depends on scope, but you're never waiting months for a single reveal.

What tools do you use?

Snowflake or BigQuery for the warehouse, dbt for transformation, and Sigma, Hex, or Looker for dashboards — whichever fits what your team already knows. See the full list above.

Do you sign NDAs and handle our data securely?

Yes — NDAs and data processing agreements are standard practice for us, not a special request. See our privacy policy for how we handle data on this site itself.

Do you work with companies outside the US?

Yes. We're remote-first, based in Honduras (CST), which overlaps every US time zone's core hours, and we work in fully fluent English.

What happens after the project ends — are we locked in?

No. You get full documentation and recorded walkthroughs, and you own the warehouse, models, and pipelines outright. Plenty of clients come back for new work, but nothing here requires it.
