Is the Blueprint the right starting point?

It is designed for consequential reporting problems, not for selling you the most complicated technology.

  • An important revenue or reporting question remains unanswered.

Common questions

Everything you should know before getting started.

What does a project actually cost?

The Revenue Data Blueprint is a fixed $2,000 and is credited in full toward implementation if you hire us to build. It includes a complete assessment, source inventory, data quality and reporting review, recommended architecture, prioritized roadmap, recorded findings presentation, and fixed-price implementation proposal. You keep the Blueprint whether or not we work together afterward.

How long does an engagement take?

The Revenue Data Blueprint is delivered within five business days of kickoff and receipt of the required access. If we move into implementation, we deliver work in weekly milestones so you can review progress as it happens. Total implementation time depends on the agreed scope.

Do you sign NDAs and handle our data securely?

Yes. We sign NDAs and data processing agreements before access is provided, when required. We work in infrastructure you control, request only the access needed to complete the project, and remove our access at handoff. See our privacy policy for how we handle data on this site.

What happens after the project ends — are we locked in?

No. You own the warehouse, models, pipelines, dashboards, and documentation we build. Your team can operate everything independently after handoff. If you want continued support, enhancements, or new reporting initiatives, we're happy to stay involved — but you'll never be locked in.

What happens if the Blueprint identifies a simple solution?

We'll recommend the smallest solution that reliably answers your business question. If that means a spreadsheet, a dashboard, or a lightweight reporting process instead of a full data platform, that's exactly what we'll recommend. Our goal is dependable decision-making — not unnecessary complexity.

What tools do you use?

We work with Snowflake or BigQuery for the warehouse, dbt for transformation, and Sigma, Hex, Looker, or Tableau for reporting. Every recommendation is based on your business, existing systems, and internal capabilities — not a predefined technology stack.

Do you manage advertising campaigns?

No. We build the data infrastructure, attribution models, and reporting layer that measure marketing performance. Your internal team or marketing agency continues managing campaigns, media buying, SEO, creative, and optimization.

Do you work with companies outside the US?

Yes. We work remotely with companies across North America and other regions. All communication, documentation, and project delivery are conducted in fluent English.

Will anyone outside the two founders work on our project?

Not without telling you first. If a project would benefit from a specialist, we explain who they are, why they are needed, and what access they would receive before involving them.
